About StyleMe AI

Our Smart AI Wardrobe is a revolutionary product that scans your clothes and your body, asks you a couple of questions, and matches your outfit for whatever reasons you want to go out. It reduces hassle with smart technology and is connected to an app that provides an overview of your wardrobe. The app also acts as an AI stylist, helping you style your clothes by allowing you to take pictures of your clothes and upload them.
